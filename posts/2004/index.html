Author found that the integer and floating scores are only related to the processor frequency and processor framework rather than other environments, such as virtual machines. Therefore, the Dhrystone 2 and Whetstone scores are used as the indicators for instruction per clock(IPC).
Remarks：Due to temperature control, the multi-core efficiency of the Android platform is not credible
SD is the abbreviation of Snapdragon Qualcomm
RM TC means remove temperature control
Remarks：LinuxDeploy 2.60 / Debian buster
Virtual machine environment：Windows LTSC 2019 / VMware Workstation Pro 16.1.2.8432 / Debian buster
The Native environment is debian 10 or 11
